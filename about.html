About BudgetFlow

BudgetFlow is a simple and smart budgeting web application designed to help individuals and small businesses manage their finances more effectively.

What is BudgetFlow?

BudgetFlow helps users track expenses, plan budgets, analyze spending habits, and stay financially organized. It is built for:

  • Students
  • Parents
  • Freelancers
  • Business owners

Key Features

  • Budget planning & tracking
  • Expense tracking by category
  • Built-in calculator
  • Notes for financial planning
  • Visual analytics & charts

How It Works

BudgetFlow runs entirely in your browser. All data is stored locally on your device, meaning your information stays private and is never uploaded to a server.

Who Made This?

BudgetFlow was created as a personal project by Pordi to promote better financial awareness and digital budgeting skills.
