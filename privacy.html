Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

BudgetFlow respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled when using the application.

Information Collection

BudgetFlow does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All data entered into the app (budgets, expenses, notes) is stored locally in your browser.

Cookies

BudgetFlow does not use cookies for tracking or advertising purposes.

Third-Party Services

BudgetFlow may use third-party libraries (such as chart libraries or icons) that operate entirely on the client side. These services do not collect personal data through this app.

Data Security

Since data is stored locally on your device, you are responsible for keeping your device secure. Clearing browser storage may delete your data.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated in the future. Any changes will be reflected on this page.

Contact
